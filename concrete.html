Concrete and Brick Pressure Washing

Deep Cleaning for Patios, Sidewalks, Driveways and More.

At WaterWerks, We Restore Your Concrete to It's Former Glory

Concrete and brick surfaces are built to last—but over time, they collect dirt, grime, algae, moss, and oil stains that can make them look worn and neglected.

By using pressure and distance, we remove grime without damaging paint, finishes, or structural integrity. This service not only improves the appearance of your railing but also helps maintain its safety by preventing slippery buildup and surface deterioration.

What’s Included in Our Concrete Pressure Washing Service?

Our powerful surface cleaners and pressure systems safely cut through tough stains on:

  • Driveways
  • Sidewalks
  • Patio
  • Brick steps and walkway
  • Walls

With a commitment to safely clean, we take all precautions to protect the material while delivering a deep and even clean. The result is a fresh, bright finish that makes your property look polished and well-maintained.
